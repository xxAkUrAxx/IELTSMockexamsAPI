Writing Test Instructions

  • Total Time: 60 minutes
  • Tasks: 2 (Task 1 and Task 2)
  • Time Management: You are responsible for managing your own time. It is recommended to spend approximately 20 minutes on Task 1 and 40 minutes on Task 2.
  • Typing Responses: You will type your answers directly into the provided text fields to the right of the screen.
  • Word Count: A word count is displayed on the bottom right of the screen to help you monitor the length of your responses.
  • Navigation: You can navigate between tasks using the on-screen interface.
  • Highlighting and Notes: You have the ability to highlight text and make notes during the test.
  • Timer: A timer is visible on the screen, counting down the remaining time.
